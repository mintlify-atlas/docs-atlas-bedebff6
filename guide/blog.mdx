The Roots blog is a public section of the platform where administrators publish articles, insights, and updates about tourism sustainability in the Barra and Costa Nova territory. No login is required to read blog posts.

## Blog listing

Navigate to `/blog` to see all published articles. Each post card in the listing shows:

- **Thumbnail image** — a visual preview of the article.
- **Title** — the article headline.

Posts are displayed in reverse chronological order with the most recent articles at the top.

## Reading a post

Click any post card to open the full article at `/blog/:postId`. The post page displays:

- **Title** — the article headline.
- **Thumbnail** — the cover image.
- **Content** — the full article body, rendered from rich HTML produced by the platform's text editor.
- **Attachments** — any files attached to the post by the administrator (for example, PDF reports or datasets).

<Note>
Post content is rendered from the output of the Tiptap rich-text editor. Formatting such as headings, bold text, lists, links, and embedded images is preserved exactly as the author wrote it.
</Note>

## Blog on the home page

A preview of the most recent posts also appears in the **Blog section** at the bottom of the [home page](/guide/home). Click any card in that preview to go directly to the full post.

## Admin access

Only users with administrator roles can create, edit, or delete blog posts. Administration of the blog is handled through the admin panel at `/admin/blog`. If you need to publish an article, contact your platform administrator.

<Info>
If you are an administrator and want to manage blog content, see the [blog management guide](/admin/blog-management) in the Admin Guide.
</Info>
