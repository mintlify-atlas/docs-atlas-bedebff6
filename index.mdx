## The root of better tourism decisions

Roots is a web platform built for tourism stakeholders, researchers, and local administrators who need reliable, up-to-date data to understand how the Barra and Costa Nova territory is evolving. The platform aggregates tourism sustainability indicators across multiple domains — from environmental impact to social and economic metrics — and presents them as interactive charts and structured data.

## Platform structure

The platform is organized around a hierarchy of concepts:

| Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| **Domain** | A top-level thematic area (e.g., Environment, Economy, Society). Each domain groups related indicators. |
| **Dimension / Subdomain** | A sub-category within a domain that narrows the scope of its indicators. |
| **Indicator** | A measurable metric with a name, unit, periodicity, and associated time-series data. |
| **Resource** | A data source attached to one or more indicators, with a defined coverage period. |

<Note>
The platform is available in Portuguese and English. Language detection is automatic based on your browser settings, and you can switch languages at any time using the language selector in the navigation bar.
</Note>
